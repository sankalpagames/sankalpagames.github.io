1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy is issued by Vasilev Valentin, IE ("Sankalpa Games"). We value your privacy and are committed to using only the minimum amount of data necessary to improve our products. 2. Information We Use

2.1. Technical Information:

To improve our Apps, we process technical information such as device type, operating system, language settings, device and advertising identifiers, approximate (coarse) location derived from your IP address, and crash and diagnostic data.

2.2. Analytics and Advertising Data:

We analyze app usage and performance data to identify trends and optimize the user experience. Advertising data may be used to deliver personalized ads, depending on your device settings.

3. How We Use Information

  • To enhance the functionality and usability of the Apps.
  • To monitor app performance and improve user experience.
  • To deliver relevant advertising, where applicable.

4. Our Commitment to Privacy

We do not operate our own servers and do not directly collect or store your personal information (such as name, email, or contact details).

Depending on the specific App, we use some or all of the following third-party services for advertising, analytics, and leaderboards:

  • Google AdMob — to display and personalize advertisements.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — for app analytics and performance monitoring.
  • LootLocker — in Apps that include leaderboards, to store and display scores under an anonymous player identifier. No account or login is required.

These partners may automatically collect technical and device data — including advertising identifiers — and process it on their own servers under their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how your data is handled:

  • Google Privacy Policy (AdMob and Firebase)
  • LootLocker Privacy Policy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below this age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us or our partners, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
